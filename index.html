Shuai Wang

Deep Learning, Autonomous Driving, Medical Image Processing

2021 - 2023
I'm the senior engineer at Riemann Lab, 2012 Lab, Huawei. My work is mainly about autonomous driving perception and HD/SD Map construction.
2019 - 2021
I was a research engineer at BioMind. The projects I was deeply involved in contain predicting functional outcome in patients with acute brainstem infarctions, cerebral anotomy segmentation, cerebral hemorrhage segmentation, brain CT registration, postoperative image recognition, skull fracture detection and segmentation, etc. It's my pleasure to work with Zhenzhou Wu, Pan Liu, Yingchen Yu, Pingye Zhang.
2016 - 2019
I received my M.S. degree in Information Technology from The University of Melbourne. This is where I first got into machine learning and deep learning. Statistical Machine Learning, AI Planning for Autonomy, Knowledge Technologies and Algorithms and Complexity are my favorite courses. I had the pleasure to do research work supervised by Trevor Cohn and Matthias Petri.
2012 - 2016
I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Minzu University of China. I wrote my first 'hello world' program here. Spent four years on studying how computer works. I joined the Computer Game Lab in 2015 for studying Doudizhu game supervised by Licheng Wu and Xiali Li.
